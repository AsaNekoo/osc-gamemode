# OSC-Gamemode

Sends your current Geometry Dash gamemode over OSC to any listener - built for syncing with VRChat avatars, but works with any OSC-compatible client.

## Settings

- **Path** – the OSC address messages are sent to (default: `/avatar/parameters/gamemode`)
- **Port** – the local UDP port to send to (default: `9000`)

## Use with VRChat

You can find prefab in the github releases of this mod. Requires VRChat to have OSC support enabled. Icons are included in the prefab, but you can replace them with your own if you want.
